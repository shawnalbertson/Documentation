$$h = u + p \upsilon$$
h -> specific enthalpy [kJ/kg]
u -> specific internal energy [kJ/kg]
$\upsilon$ -> specific volume [m^3/kg]

$$H = U + p V$$
H -> enthalpy [kJ]
U -> internal energy [kJ]
V -> volume [m^3]
