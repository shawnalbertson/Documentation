## Ideal gas

## Incompressible
- Density does not vary with pressure
	- As a result, $c_v = c_p$
- Internal energy depends only on temperature
	- $u(T,P) \approx u(T)$

Change in enthalpy
$$h_2 - h_1 = c(T_2 - T_1) + \upsilon(P_2-P_1)$$

Change in internal energy
$$ u_2 - u_1 = c(T_2 - T_1)$$
